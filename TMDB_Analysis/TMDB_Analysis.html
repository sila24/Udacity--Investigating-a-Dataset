Introduction¶

In this project, I will analyze TMDB Movie Data which contains about 1000 movies, this data is originally from Kaggle. I would be cleaning and investigating the data to draw conclusions on the questions listed below:

  1. Which movies have the highest and lowest budget?
  2. What is the average budget per year?
  3. Which movies have the highest and lowest revenue?
  4. What is the average revenue per year?
  5. What is the most and least popular movie?
  6. Do movies with higher budget gain more popularity?
  7. Do movies with higher popularity gain more profit?
  8. Do movies with higher popularity gain more votes?
  9. Which actor has starred in the most movies?
  10. What is top acted genre of movie?
  11. Which genres are most popular from year to year?

def calculate(column):
    #for highest 
    high= df_tmdb[column].idxmax()
    higher=pd.DataFrame(df_tmdb.loc[high])
    
    #for lowest 
    low= df_tmdb[column].idxmin()
    lower=pd.DataFrame(df_tmdb.loc[low])
    
    #collectin data in one place
    info=pd.concat([higher, lower], axis=1)
    
    return info
